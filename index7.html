SUNSET AT RMIT

/"Sunset at RMIT" is my personal project serving for my own hobby in photography. I often use my freetime to wander around and capture some moments that I find beautiful and gorgeous. Sunset is extremely precious, especially at my university./

/Most of the pictures are captured in basketball court by my FUJIFILM XS10 camera./

/The moment of sunset always makes me feel peaceful and be myself. I love the green color of trees and grass, yellow and orange colors of sun and sky. Everything in the pictures is absolutely priceless./

/I'm grateful that I can relax myself by my hobby at my school./
