DARKROOM

/"Darkroom" is the 3D rendering project of the course "Digital Media Studio 1: Production" in the design program. For the final product, the students were required to model a 3D fantasy environment./

/My idea for that project is to create a Victorian darkroom of nature./

/The project gives me opportunities to practice 3D modelling skills, creativity and innovation in design thinking, take use of the modern software to create what I imagine./
