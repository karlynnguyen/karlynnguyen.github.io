JUMPING AROUND

/"Jumping around" is the first assignment of the course "Digital Media Studio 2: Collaboration" in the major's program. The idea of this assessment is to create 8 vector icons to display the theme "Travel Attractions"./

/My 8 icons represent famous locations in the world, which are: Statue of Liberty, The Opera House, Namsan Tower, Mount Fuji, Iho Tewoo Beach, Big Ben, Eiffel Tower, Gyeongbokgung Palace./

/Through this project, I have an ability to practice creative and strategic design thinking, consider consistences between designs and color palletes./
